# User Guide

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Click **Sign Up** in the top navigation bar
2. Choose your role: **Tenant** (looking for a house) or **Landlord** (listing a property)
3. Enter your name, phone number (`+254XXXXXXXXX`), and a password (min 6 characters)
4. Click **Create Account** — you'll be logged in immediately

### Tenant Workflow

#### Finding a Property

1. Go to **Explore** to browse all available listings
2. Use the **Filter by** dropdown to narrow by house type (Bedsitter, 1-Bed, etc.)
3. Enter a **price** to show properties within ±KES 1,000 of your target
4. Each card shows: rent, deposit, distance from you, amenities, and house type

#### Viewing Property Details

Click **View Details** on any property card to see:

- Full description, amenities, and location
- **Market Fairness Analysis** — how the rent compares to similar properties nearby
- **Standard Repair Deductions** — the landlord's pre-set repair costs
- **Photo gallery** — images uploaded by the landlord
- **Tenancy Agreement** — download the landlord's agreement document
- **Property location on a map** — click "View on Map" for a detailed popup

#### Contacting a Landlord

1. On the property detail page, click **Pay with M-Pesa** (KES 99)
2. Once payment is confirmed, click **Unlock Contact**
3. The landlord's phone number will be revealed with a **Chat on WhatsApp** button
4. WhatsApp opens with a pre-filled message about the property

#### Submitting a Review

After unlocking a property, you can submit a review indicating:
- Was the listing fraudulent?
- Is the property occupied?
- Did the landlord demand extra fees?
- Any additional comments

> Properties with 3+ fraud reports are automatically flagged and hidden from search.

### Landlord Workflow

#### Posting a Listing

1. Click **Post Listing** in the navigation
2. **Step 1 — Details**: House type, rent, deposit, title, description, location, coordinates
3. **Step 2 — Amenities**: Check infrastructure features (borehole, WiFi, hot shower, etc.)
4. **Step 3 — Repair Rates**: Add itemised repair costs for deposit deductions
5. **Step 4 — Photos & Agreement**: Upload property images, videos, and your tenancy agreement document
6. Click **Create Listing** — the property starts in **Pending Pay** status

#### Activating a Listing

1. Go to your **Dashboard**
2. Find the listing with a yellow "PENDING PAY" badge
3. Click **Pay KES 299 to Activate**
4. Once paid, the status changes to **ACTIVE** and the property appears on Explore

#### Managing Listings

The **Dashboard** shows all your listings with:
- Rent, unlock count, and unique tenant views
- Repair rates for each property
- Status badges (Active, Pending Pay, Flagged, Inactive)
- A **View Public Listing** link for active properties

#### Your Profile

Go to **Profile** to:
- Update your profile picture (hover over the avatar and click "Change")
- View your business name and account details
- See all your listings
